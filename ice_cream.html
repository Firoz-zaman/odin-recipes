RECIPE FOR ICE CREAM

  1. Step 1 In a bowl, mix all ingredients until sugar dissolves.
  2. Step 2 Pour into a shallow dish and put it in the freezer.
  3. Step 3 Every 30–45 minutes, stir or whisk vigorously to break up ice crystals. Repeat for 3–4 hours.
  4. Step 4 Freeze overnight for a smooth finish.
