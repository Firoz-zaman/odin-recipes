RECIPE FOR CAKE

  1. Step 1 Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. Step 2 In a bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Step 3 Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Step 4 Mix in the vanilla extract.
  5. Step 5 In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Step 6 Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture, alternating with milk, until smooth.X
  7. Step 7 Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Step 8 Let the cake cool, then slice and serve as is — or frost if desired.
